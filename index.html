Greetings! Welcome to Sydney Machine Learning (SML) Group!

This page is set up to facilitate the members of the group to read relevant papers and conduct efficient academic exchanges. And everyone can collect papers in the research area of ​​interest and the reproduction codes of the papers. Work together and make progress!

Our research interests lie in providing mathematical and theoretical foundations to justify and understand machine learning models and designing efficient learning algorithms for problems in computer vision and data mining, with a particular emphasis on

  • Statistical learning theory (e.g., hypothesis complexity and generalisation error)
  • Weakly supervised learning (e.g., transfer learning, learning with label noise, positive and unlabeled learning)
  • Adversarial learning (e.g., adversarial attack and defense)
  • Unsupervised learning (e.g., clustering and matrix factorisation)
  • Image and video processing (e.g., classification)
